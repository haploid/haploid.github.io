Haploid LLC

Haploid LLC is a robotics software company in Brooklyn, NY.

We aim to make artists, designers, engineers, and architects more than human by developing deeper connections between them and the machines that help realize their work.

We reject the traditional digital fabrication pipeline which neglects to extend the expressiveness of digital design tools to physical fabrication processes.

We propose instead the concept of Robotically Aided Design (RAD), where the creator and their tools are co-participants in design and production.
